What is AEGEE?

AEGEE is a large European network that acts in more than 200 cities across Europe with around 13 000 members. Together, we organize Summer Universities, working groups and conferences striving for international cooperation and friendship. AEGEE is open for all university students.

In AEGEE-Helsinki, we regularly organize local activities like excursions, events, discussions, parties and cultural exchanges where we visit other AEGEE locals. We want to give the opportunity for Finnish and international students to get to know each other!
